🟢 Value Added (VA)
Kaam jo seedha customer ya product ke liye value create karta hai. Jitna zyada green, utna better. Target: 50%+
🔵 Business Necessary (BNVA)
Zaruri kaam — compliance, filing, reports. Value nahi banata lekin band nahi kar sakte. Isko minimize karo. Target: minimize
🔴 Non-Value Added (NVA)
Pure waste — corrections, duplicate kaam, approvals ka wait. Ye system ki failure hai. Target: below 20%
🟣 Unidentified Time
420 min mein se jo log nahi hua. Agar zyada hai — form fill nahi hua ya kaam track nahi hua.
⏱ 420 Minutes Formula
1 working day = 7 hours = 420 minutes
VA mins + BNVA mins + NVA mins = Logged time
420 − Logged time = Unidentified
Each % = (Category mins ÷ 420) × 100
