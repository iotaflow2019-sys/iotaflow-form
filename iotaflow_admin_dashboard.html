💡 Ye Dashboard Kya Hai aur Kaise Kaam Karta Hai
🎯 Idea kya hai?
Iotaflow mein har employee roz ek simple form fill karta hai — "aaj maine kya kiya." Ye dashboard us data ko automatically collect karke ek jagah dikhata hai taaki hum samajh sakein ki team ka time kahan ja raha hai.
⏱ 420 Minutes — Hamari Currency
Ek working day = 420 minutes. Har activity ka time is 420 mein se count hota hai. Bacha hua time "Unidentified" mein jaata hai — matlab wo time capture nahi hua.
🟢 Green — Value Added (VA)
Wo kaam jo seedha customer ya product ke liye kuch banata hai. Jitna zyada green, utna achha — iska matlab team productive hai aur sahi direction mein kaam kar rahi hai.
🔵 Blue — Business Necessary (BNVA)
Zaruri kaam jo karna padta hai — jaise compliance, filing, payroll. Ye value toh nahi banata lekin band nahi kar sakte. Isko minimize karna target hona chahiye.
🔴 Red — Non-Value Added (NVA)
Pure waste — corrections, approvals ka intezaar, duplicate kaam. Ye system ki galti hai, employee ki nahi. Isko dekhkar hum samajhte hain kahan fix karna hai.
🟣 Purple — Unidentified Time
420 minutes mein se jo time log nahi hua — pata nahi kahaan gaya. Agar ye zyada hai toh ya toh form fill nahi hua ya koi kaam track nahi kiya gaya.
🔄 Dashboard Ko Use Kaise Karein
Auto Refresh
Dashboard khud har 5 minute mein data update karta hai. Kuch karna nahi padta.
Manual Refresh
Upar "↻ Refresh" button dabao ya sidebar mein green button dabao — turant latest data aa jayega.
Employee Form Link
Sidebar mein "Employee Form" button hai — click karo, seedha form khulega naye tab mein.
Google Sheet
Sidebar ya topbar mein "Google Sheet" button hai — raw data seedha sheet mein dikhega.
